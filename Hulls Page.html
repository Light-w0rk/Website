Tanki Online Hulls



Light Hulls

Wasp
Fast, with negligible armor, wasp can outrun any other hull. Its high speed and maneuverability take skill to control, but allow it to perform well on cramped maps. Great with short-range guns.


Hornet
A light hull for those who want speed without completely trading off it for armor. With its drift feature that allows it to skid around corners, Hornet is a tough hull to maneuver but is nonetheless very steady. Looks great with any gun.



Medium Hulls

Hunter
The most verstile hull in the game. It has decent speed for a medium tank and enough armor to handle itself well in battles at any range. Goes well with almost any gun and is a real hunter!


Viking
A real Viking: wide and incredibly stable. Average movement and maneuverability, but enviable armor. Works well with mid and long-range guns. Its incredibly low hull makes a hard target.


Dictator
Being the biggest and tallest hull in the game, Dictator inspires fear and commands respect. Its height allows it a higher vantage point from which to fire. On the flip side, this makes it a bigger target. It lacks speed, but has multiple armor plates to make up for it. Works well when outfitted with Railgun.


Heavy Hulls

Titan
The name says it all: heavy armor, low top speed, tons of weight and power. A durable hull, ideal for those who want to last longer in skirmishes. Pairs beautifully with long-range guns.



Mammoth
There is no match for this one's armor, although your grandmother would beat it in a race. A fortress of a tank, chosen by those who are in no hurry and prefer to shrug off anything the enemy throws at them. Works best with long-range guns.
