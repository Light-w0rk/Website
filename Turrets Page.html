Tanki Online Turrets



Close Combat Turrets

Firebird
One of the best weapons for short-ranged battles. Fires a steady stream of napalm that burns and melts tanks within its damage radius. Burning opponents continue to take damage even after the Firebird's attack stops. Can thaw frozen tanks caused by Freeze and is a great choice for fast and medium hulls.


Freeze
A unique weapon for short-range battles. Slows opponents by freezing them and can put out fires caused by Firebird. In the right hands, it can destroy an enemy without receiving any damage in return. Great for fast and medium hulls.


Isida
An essential gun in team battles. Can heal teammates within its firing radius and effectively destroy opponents at short-range. Repairs its own hull while damaging enemies. Works well with any hull.



Medium Range Turrets

Ricochet
A gun that can be tough to control. Ideal for short to mid-range battles. Possesses a unique feature in that its shells ricochet off walls, allowing it to shoot around corners. Just make sure you don’t fire a round that comes ricocheting right back at you! Goes well with all hulls.


Twins
A gun with unlimited firing duration. Effective at short and medium ranges. Its high rate of fire makes it ideal for knocking off the aim from enemies. Works well with any hull.


Hammer
Perfect choice for close and medium ranged combat. Three powerful shots in your clip means your opponents have no way out. Its shotgun festure allows Hammer to hit several targets with one shot at medium ranges. Works best with medium hulls.



Long Range Turrets

Vulcan
A turret with an unlimited firing rate. It is best suited for combat at mid and long ranges. Vulcan has a unique gyroscope feature, which makes knocking off its aim very challenging, even when it's under heavy enemy fire. Caution — When using Vulcan, firing continuously for an extended time may overheat and damage your tank! Ideal for use with heavy hulls.


Smoky
A gun for mid to long-range battles. Fires single shells and can land critical hits. Works well with all hulls.


Railgun
A gun for professionals. Performs well at any range. Takes a long time to reload and needs to warm up before firing, but packs a hefty punch. A good pairing for any hull, but light tanks run the risk of flipping over from the recoil! A favorite amongst clan players.


Shaft
A great choice for a sniper. It is the only gun with an optical scope attached, allowing it to easily hit targets at long range. Can also fire short-range without using the scope. Works well with any hull, but light hulls risk flipping over from the recoil!


Thunder
A great choice for mid-range battles. Rounds from Thunder explode on impact and damage everything within their blast radius. This makes it risky to use at short-range as it may inflict self-damage. Goes well with any hull.
